What Do We Do

Alternative Education is a Completely Open and Non-Hierarchical Community. Our Mission is to bring together Individuals in the Alternative Education Space and to awake Questioning about the fundamental issues in education. We Intend to be a Starting Point on your Search to Explore Alternatives in Education. We are a Democratic Community and your inputs reflect How the community is structured. Feel free to use the Get Involved Page Or the Chat Group Page to Give in your Ideas and Solutions as to how we should move forward.
